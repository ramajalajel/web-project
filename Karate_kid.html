The Karate Kid

Writer: Robert Mark Kamen

Director: John G. Avildsen

Release Year: 1984

IMDb Rating: 7.3/10

Plot Summary:
The Karate Kid is a sports drama film that follows the story of Daniel LaRusso, a young teenager who moves to a new town and struggles to adjust to his new life. After being bullied, he meets his mentor, Mr. Miyagi, who teaches him karate not only for self-defense but also to achieve inner balance and life discipline.

Key Characters:
Daniel LaRusso (Ralph Macchio) – the young protagonist striving to overcome challenges.
Mr. Miyagi (Pat Morita) – the wise mentor who teaches Daniel valuable life lessons.

This film became one of the most iconic inspirational films of the 80s and continues to influence generations.
